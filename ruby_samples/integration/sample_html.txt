This is the basic structure of the way Google News stories
are marked up, with unimportant things edited out.

<div class="headline-story">
  <h2 class="title">
    <a href="url">title text (with search term highlighted)</a>
  </h2>
  <div class="sub-title">
    <span class="source">publication name</span>
    <span class="date">how long ago</span>
  </div>
  <div class="body">
    <div class="snippet">snippet text</div>
  </div>
</div>
